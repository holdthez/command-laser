My Bio

Michael Few has spent the last 15 years deeply committed to understanding the optimization of health, well-being, leadership development, regeneration, and human performance. As a strategic designer and creative thinker, Michael understands the importance of marketing and brand objectives, consumer needs, and value of effective communications.

With a passion for storytelling, he helps people figure out what they need to say and how to say it, implementing the elements of an impactful narrative that moves and inspires people. He enjoys helping clients cut through the noise and tell their stories in a clear and simple way, connecting with viewers on an emotional level, and weaving their narrative into content, pictures, and graphics to engage and inform the viewer.
